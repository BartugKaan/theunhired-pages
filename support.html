Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > "Restore Purchases" in the app. If you previously purchased the full game, it will be restored on your current device. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Make sure your device is running iOS 17.6 or later
  • Close the app completely and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Delete and reinstall the app (your purchase can be restored)

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings > Language in the app. We support 8 languages: English, Turkish, German, Spanish, French, Italian, and Portuguese (Portugal & Brazil). After changing, the app will ask you to restart.

Is my game progress saved?

Your progress (scores, career points, settings) is saved locally on your device. If you delete the app, your progress will be lost. We recommend taking a screenshot of your stats before uninstalling.

How do I unlock other characters?

Characters can be unlocked by earning Career Points through gameplay, or by purchasing the full game to unlock all characters instantly.

I was charged but didn't get Premium. What do I do?

Try restoring your purchase first (Settings > Restore Purchases). If that doesn't work, contact us via email below. Please include your Apple ID email and the approximate date of purchase.
