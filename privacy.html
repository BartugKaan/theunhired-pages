THE UNHIRED

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

The Unhired ("we", "our", "the app") is a satirical mobile game developed by Bartug Kaan Celebi. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how we handle data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect anonymous usage data to improve the game experience. This includes:

  • Game events (game start, game end, level up, card swipe)
  • Minigame performance (type played, score tier)
  • App crashes and performance metrics (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Device type and OS version (anonymous, aggregated)

This data is anonymous and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users. Firebase Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Game Center

The app integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not access your Apple ID or Game Center profile data.

In-App Purchases

The app offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium content. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Crash reporting
  • Apple Game Center — Leaderboards & achievements
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases

Children's Privacy

The Unhired is rated 12+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains satirical humor about job hunting and career struggles.

Data Storage

Game progress (scores, settings, career points) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data is not transmitted to any server and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can reset your in-app analytics identifier through your device's Settings > Privacy > Analytics.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL. We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top.
